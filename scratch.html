Cows, Cattle, and Bovine

This page is all about cows...

slay


  1. Cows
    • Cows have a great sense of smell and can smell something up to six miles away.
    • Cows have a panoramic field of vision of about 300 degrees and can see color.
    • Cows have best friends and they tend to spend most of their time together.
  2. Cattle
    • Cattle have a unique digestive system with four compartments in their stomach.
    • Cattle are social animals and develop strong bonds with other members of their herd.
    • Cattle can run at speeds of up to 25 miles per hour when they are scared or excited.
  3. Bovine
    • The term "bovine" refers to the scientific family that includes cattle, buffalo, bison, and yak.
    • Bovines have a complex social hierarchy, with dominant and submissive individuals within the herd.
    • Bovines have a strong maternal instinct and will fiercely protect their young from predators.
